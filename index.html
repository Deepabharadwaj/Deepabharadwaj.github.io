About

[ 03 / Bio ]
DEEPA BHARADWAJ

From architecture to robotics infrastructure.

I'm a co-founder, product builder, and designer with a background that spans architecture, UX, product management, and robotics infrastructure.

Before Proxy, I was a PM at Intrinsic (Alphabet's robotics company), where I worked on tooling for deploying physical robotic workcells. Earlier, I led product at Steelcase's Digital Innovation Lab, where my team shipped an AI-powered configurator that drove over $100M in revenue impact.

I hold an MBA from the Stanford Graduate School of Business (Class of 2026) and two architecture degrees from the University of Michigan.

The throughline across architecture, product management, and founding Proxy has been the same: figuring out how to make complex, physical systems usable by the people who actually need them.
