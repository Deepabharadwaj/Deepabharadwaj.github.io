Deepa Bharadwaj

Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I’m currently working at a design firm in San Francisco, and graduated from the University Of Michigan with an M.Arch in 2016.

Having studied and practiced architectural design for over 10 years, I’m obsessed with designing and choreographing experiences for people, in both the physical and digital realms.

I discovered UX design during research for a filmmaking class I was taking at U of M, and was intrigued. I worked for two years at two different architecture firms in Chicago and San Francisco, and realized I was looking for more design flexibility, iterative capacity, and direct interaction with the users/clients. I took a UXUI bootcamp at UC Berkeley, and absolutely loved what I was doing.

I’m currently looking for an opportunity to work as a UX designer in a super collaborative and fun environment, understanding & designing human experiences is my jam.

Outside of work, I am an adrenaline junkie- I’ve gone skydiving, paragliding, parasailing- jumping off the Grand Canyon is next!
