The Challenge

Dog parents need peace of mind that their pets can be found quickly and efficiently if they wander away from home. The American Humane Association estimates that 1 out of 3 pets become lost at some point in their lifetime and according to the Coalition for Reuniting Pets and Families, less than 23% of lost pets in the U.S. are reunited with their owners.
Problem Statement
How might we give pet owners peace of mind on the safety and security of their dog’s well-being and help reunite any lost dogs with their worried owners as quickly as possible?

Research

Affinity Mapping and Feature Brainstorming

We collaborated on Zoom, to brainstorm potential features for the app- performing affinity mapping & voting on prioritizing the features to be implemented.

Based on our Affinity Mapping and Feature Prioritization Matrix, we honed in on these key features to be implemented in the Bark App:
Track Live Location
  • Track Anywhere, Anytime
  • Set Safe Zone
  • Share your dog's location
Alerts
  • Escape Detection
  • Time to Walk. Feed & Play
Health Monitor
  • Heart Rate
  • Activity Levels
  • Sleep Pattern

Ideation

User Flow
Based on our affinity mapping, we charted out a User Flow for the following tasks:
  • Introduction to App
  • User Account Creation
  • Dog Profile Setup
  • Homepage Dashboard

Wireframing

Lo-Fidelity Wireframing
As a team, we came up with lo-fidelity sketches for different screens based on our task flow. We then collaborated to combine the screens/ best feature to design in high fidelity prototyping.

Branding &
Identity

Custom Illustration & Logo
We worked on Illustrating a key 'mascot' to guide the user through the process, and make it more engaging and fun.

Style Guide

We researched the UI style of Leverege, and drew inspiration from their simple, bright, playful colors to design and brand the Bark app interface.

Hi-Fidelity Wireframing

We applied our Style Guide to the Lo-Fidelity Mockups, and split up the screens per project member due to the short time frame. We designed the onboarding, homepage, as well as dog profile pages.

Hi-Fidelity Prototype

We fixed the minor UI and visual design issues discovered through usability testing and spent most of our time working on prototyping the onboarding process since this was such a critical piece of our value proposition.
