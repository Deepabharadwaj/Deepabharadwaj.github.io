01 · Problem

Complexity was costing deals.

Users spent enormous amounts of time navigating the complexity of Steelcase's product portfolio and customizing 3D models per their customer's requests.

Ease of doing business is key to retaining customers — and the existing platform was frustrating enough that the company was losing projects to it.

If we couldn't make customization fast, we'd keep losing deals to teams that already had.

02 · Solution

Next-gen product visualization.

Under the theme of Next-Gen Product Visualization, we asked: could we build an experience where a sales rep, dealer, or customer could find a product and customize it directly in the browser — no installs, no specialists, no back-and-forth?

The answer became a web-based configurator powered by a generative-design engine. Pick a product, change the variables that matter — height, finish, fabric, footprint — and watch the 3D model regenerate in real time.
